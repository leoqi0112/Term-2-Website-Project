Gameplay

The game starts off with you creating your own character and selecting from six unique starting farm maps. You start off owning a small plot of land with a small house. At the start players must clear out the wilderness to run the farm. They would start by tending crops and livestocks and generating money off of them; the revenue they earn could be used to expand farm facilities. Later in game, there could also be interactions with other players in various settings.
